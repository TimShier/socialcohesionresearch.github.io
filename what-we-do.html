What we do

Understand our process

Social Cohesion Research employs a mixture of technological excellence and years of research experience to deliver some of the most innovative and informative research available. Vibrand Research, with over 12 years of proven market research, provides the intellectual and organisational support to Social Cohesion Research.

Over those 12 years we’ve tried and tested various research methods. We know what works, and what doesn’t. We draw on that knowledge and experience in our work on social issues. As Social Cohesion Research we run various projects, on a variety of issues. When we conduct research, we:

  • Utilise our network of research partners in Africa and the Middle East
  • Employ cutting-edge technology in our work
  • Partner with specialist consultants with relevant knowledge
  • Make use of our years of research experience
  • Retain an openness to learning and curiosity about what we may learn.

We aim to use research and knowledge creation for social good. We provide primary data to organisations or individuals who may need it. Our clients include NGOs, Think Tanks, Development Agencies and Foreign Aid Organisations. We develop and produce primary research. We have found that our knowledge of how to ask great questions has enabled us to conduct great research on just about anything.

We do have specialist skillsets and areas of expertise. For those that we don’t, we draw on our extensive network of subject matter experts Thus far we have conducted on issues related to health, safety, crime, violence, terrorism, extremism, refugees, migration, disease and education…. We are committed to an ongoing process of delivering usable research in the public interest. These are available here (link to publicly available reports).
