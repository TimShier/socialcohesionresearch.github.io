Bespoke Research

Bespoke research designed for you

Bespoke Research comprises most of our work stream – we provide quick, face-to-face, agile data from our 54 country Reach network. We also provide deep analytics on conversations on social media via Opinion. Our most powerful work comprises a combination of both – gathering information from citizens face-to-face and through analysing their social media conversations. Please contact us if you wish to discuss bespoke research projects.
