Who are we?

The Citizen Research Centre is a Research organisation focused on deepening our understanding of important social issues and contributing to their solutions. The Citizen Research Centre is born out of a deep commitment to adding value to the world, through utilising our years of research experience. As researchers we realised that the best way we can contribute to a better world is through generating primary data.

We describe what we do as social research. This is research done in order to improve and expand on our knowledge of the world by providing decision makers in social policy and intervention projects with the best data possible.

We run primary research - both quantitative and qualitative - in 54 countries in Africa and the Middle East. We run analytical research on social media on a global basis through our partnership with Crimson Hexagon

We work for a variety of clients, but are also committed to generating our own data for public distribution. We generate our own data using traditional, online and social media. The Citizen Research Centre was born of Vibrand Research, a market research firm with a reputation for intelligent, innovative work.
